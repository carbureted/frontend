About us

Activist to activist

Basically, we are activists working together to organize international solidarity. We are legally founded in Copenhagen,
as a non-profit, socio-economic foundation.
Currently, we are expanding and decentralising to an internatonally based organization, with open-source ambitions and local affinity groups. We will become a platform by the movements, for the movements - and continue to kick ass.
