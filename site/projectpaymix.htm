Project: Shelter

Shelter is an autonomous tech collective and was born since we saw a need to provide secure and stable infrastructure that are run with solidarity in mind rather than motivated by profit. We will provide shelter friends! We need support.

About Shelter

The people that working with Shelter have long experience working in the "engine room" of the internet, designing, constructing and maintaining systems.

We are a closed group of people that share the same political value and have known each other for many years. All our decision are made together and we have fully control over our infrastructure. Even if we are a closed group we are networked with many other collectives, groups and people around the world.

Servers for Shelter

Secure hosting is essential for serious political movements and initiatives, but activists shouldn't spend their time on servers. They should spend it on the streets. Don't just leave hosting to any specialist, leave it with other activists who will provide it for free out of pure solidarity.
