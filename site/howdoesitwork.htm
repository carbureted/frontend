How does it work?

1

Crowdfunding

Crowdfunding is simply a way of mobilizing a crowd in support of your project, and converting that support into direct funding with many small donations.

The aim is to create a tool that uses financing of initiatives for social change as a means to organize solidarity internationally.

2

Solidarity

To organize solidarity, we need to connect our struggles and support each other in reaching common goals. We aim to create a tool for initiatives to connect and support each other.

This platform is based on horizontal exchange of support and funding: Solidarity.

3

Who are you

People looking to save others, oppress others or take advantages of others, will not be given space on this platform.

This tool is created to redistribute power from the few to the many and organize global solidarity. No charity, no profits and no discrimination.

4

Rules:

Don't kick down

Don't fund on behalf of others

Be honest

5

We are a collective

A collective of activists coordinates and facilitates the platform. These are the people you talk to if you wish to organize a campaign.

We are currently working on decentralizing the work of facilitating crowdfunding campaigns and the platform itself.

6

Security

The internet is a useful tool to organize solidarity, but you never know who might be listening. We do our best to protect the data that runs through this site, but money transfers and internet traffic leaves traces.

Please use PGP encryption when you contact us, you can pay with bitcoins and you should hide your online tracks with VPN and /or Tor.

When organizing online, take care of each other, so we can be dangerous together.
